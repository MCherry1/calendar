> Calendar Jump Syntax
> 
> `!cal upcoming|Specific Year,year ?\{YYYY\}}` does not map cleanly to a month view.
> 
> Top-level `!cal`, `!cal show`, and `!cal send` jumps render whole months or years.
> 
> Use a month name, a month plus year, or a full date that includes a month:
> 
> `!cal Zarantyr`  
> `!cal Zarantyr 998`  
> `!cal Rhaan 14`  
> `!cal next month`  
> `!cal this year`
> 
> Bare day-only inputs like `!cal 14` or `!cal 1st` are not supported here.
