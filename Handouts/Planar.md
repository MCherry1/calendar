# Planar Reference

This file consolidates the editable reference material that currently lives in the planar handouts. It is intended for revising cycle notes, associated moons, and plane-effect text without digging into code.

Live/generated outputs are intentionally excluded here: no current-state summaries, no current-month overlays, and no date-specific forecasts.

## Shared Mechanics

- Canonical vs generated: canonical coterminous/remote windows come from each plane's traditional cycle. Generated shifts add sparse off-cycle events without replacing the canonical schedule.
- Anchors and seeds: long-cycle planes use seeded anchors unless the GM pins an explicit anchor year or override. This is why setup-time seeding choices matter.
- Overrides: GM overrides supersede canonical and generated states for the chosen duration, then expire back into the underlying schedule.
- Fixed planes remain in their fixed state unless explicitly overridden by the GM.

### Daanvi

- Title: The Perfect Order
- Type: cyclic
- Orbit: 400 years
- Associated moon: Nymm
- Traditional note: coterminous for a century, then remote for a century one hundred years later.
- Coterminous effect: no obvious global effect, though some sages link it to the rise of civilizations and eldritch machine rituals.
- Remote effect: no obvious global effect; the remote state itself lasts a full century.

### Dal Quor

- Title: The Region of Dreams
- Type: fixed
- Associated moon: Crya
- Traditional note: knocked off orbit during the Quori invasion of Xen'drik roughly 40,000 years ago; permanently remote.
- Remote effect: only reachable through dreaming; plane shift cannot reach Dal Quor; no manifest zones arise naturally.

### Dolurrh

- Title: The Realm of the Dead
- Type: cyclic
- Orbit: 100 years
- Associated moon: Aryth
- Traditional note: coterminous for 1 year once per century; remote for 1 year fifty years later. Shorter off-cycle shifts are tied to Aryth full/new moons through the moon-tied generator.
- Coterminous effect: ghosts slip more easily into the Material, especially near manifest zones; resurrection attempts can draw unwanted spirits.
- Remote effect: ordinary resurrection cannot pull spirits back; recovery instead requires reaching the shade in Dolurrh, and ghostly aftermath becomes more likely.

### Fernia

- Title: The Sea of Fire
- Type: cyclic
- Orbit: 5 years
- Associated moon: Eyre
- Traditional note: coterminous during Lharvion once every five years; remote during Zarantyr once every five years, exactly 2.5 years later.
- Coterminous effect: temperatures rise sharply, enabling extreme heat traits and rare transport into Fernia through intense flame.
- Remote effect: extreme heat weakens; creatures gain advantage against intense heat and against fire-damage spell saves.

### Irian

- Title: The Eternal Dawn
- Type: cyclic
- Orbit: 3 years
- Associated moon: Barrakas
- Traditional note: coterminous for 10 days in Eyre and remote for 10 days in Sypheros once every 3 years, with the remote phase 1.5 years later and a floating start day.
- Coterminous effect: life, health, fertility, and radiant energy flourish; all creatures gain advantage on saves against disease, poison, and fear.
- Remote effect: the world feels drained and dim; fear saves worsen, radiant resistance appears, and ordinary healing is halved.

### Kythri

- Title: The Churning Chaos
- Type: fixed baseline
- Associated moon: Zarantyr
- Traditional note: coterminous and remote phases are completely unpredictable, ranging from days to centuries.
- Coterminous effect: no discernible global effect on the Material Plane.
- Remote effect: no discernible global effect on the Material Plane.

### Lamannia

- Title: The Twilight Forest
- Type: cyclic
- Orbit: 1 year
- Associated moon: Olarune
- Traditional note: coterminous for a week around the summer solstice and remote for a week around the winter solstice every year.
- Coterminous effect: manifest zones intensify, fertility rises in nature, and beast/elemental duration effects are extended.
- Remote effect: fertility and animal health decline, and beast/elemental durations are reduced.

### Mabar

- Title: The Endless Night
- Type: cyclic
- Orbit: 1 year with special remote cadence
- Associated moon: Sypheros
- Traditional note: Long Shadows is coterminous for 3 days around the winter solstice every year; the remote phase lasts 5 days around the summer solstice once every 5 years.
- Coterminous effect: necrotic power spreads, light radii are halved, and night-bound darkness can open gateways.
- Remote effect: creatures gain necrotic resistance and undead become easier to turn or frighten.

### Risia

- Title: The Plain of Ice
- Type: cyclic
- Orbit: 5 years
- Associated moon: Dravago
- Traditional note: exactly opposite Fernia on the same 5-year cycle; coterminous in midwinter, remote in midsummer.
- Coterminous effect: temperatures drop sharply, enabling extreme cold traits and rare transport into Risia through intense cold.
- Remote effect: severe cold weakens; creatures gain advantage against extreme cold and against cold-damage spell saves.

### Shavarath

- Title: The Eternal Battleground
- Type: cyclic
- Orbit: 36 years
- Associated moon: Vult
- Traditional note: coterminous for 1 year every 36 years and remote for 1 year every 36 years; also has frequent single-day coterminous spikes that the remote phase suppresses.
- Coterminous effect: tempers flare, riots and violence intensify, and war magic spills broadly into the world.
- Remote effect: mostly suppresses the one-day spikes; otherwise little global effect is observed.

### Syrania

- Title: The Azure Sky
- Type: cyclic
- Orbit: 10 years
- Associated moon: Therendor
- Traditional note: coterminous on 9 Rhaan once every 10 years and remote on the same date 5 years later; Boldrei's Feast is tied to this cycle.
- Coterminous effect: goodwill spreads, skies clear, and Absolute Peace/Gentle Thoughts effects extend broadly.
- Remote effect: skies gray over, tempers worsen, persuasion suffers, intimidation strengthens, and non-manifest-zone flight slows.

### Thelanis

- Title: The Faerie Court
- Type: cyclic
- Orbit: 225 years
- Associated moon: Rhaan
- Traditional note: traditionally coterminous for 7 years every 225 years and remote for 7 years halfway between, but the cycle is believed disrupted and currently uncertain.
- Coterminous effect: gateway zones emerge, fey cross over, and accidental passage into Thelanis becomes easier around taboo-breaking.
- Remote effect: manifest-zone effects are suppressed, fey may be drawn back, and the world feels less magical.

### Xoriat

- Title: The Realm of Madness
- Type: fixed
- Associated moon: Lharvion
- Traditional note: Gatekeeper seals keep Xoriat from becoming coterminous; its remote phases are unpredictable and slow.
- Remote effect: no known global effect on the Material Plane.
