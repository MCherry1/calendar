# Lunar Reference

This file consolidates the editable reference material that currently lives in the lunar handouts. It is intended for revising moon lore, associations, and mechanics notes without digging into code.

Live/generated outputs are intentionally excluded here: no current-phase summaries, no rolling moon calendars, no forecast windows, and no date-specific eclipse results.

## Shared Mechanics

- Phase labels in the handouts use the same illumination-driven labels as the chat panels.
- Eclipses are derived from the seeded moon model and reported in the time buckets used elsewhere in the calendar UI.
- In Eberron, Long Shadows occurs when Mabar turns coterminous and the nearest new moon to the winter midpoint is claimed and darkened.

### Zarantyr

- Title: The Storm Moon
- Cycle: 27.32 days
- Associations: month of Zarantyr, Kythri, Mark of Storm
- Note: the closest and largest moon in the sky; storms are said to worsen when it is full.

### Olarune

- Title: The Sentinel Moon
- Cycle: 30.8052 days
- Associations: month of Olarune, Lamannia, Mark of Sentinel
- Note: a warm amber moon watched by druids and rangers for signs in the natural world.

### Therendor

- Title: The Healer's Moon
- Cycle: 34.735 days
- Associations: month of Therendor, Syrania, Mark of Healing
- Note: a soft silver moon tied to healing, midwifery, and carefully timed rites of care.

### Eyre

- Title: The Anvil
- Cycle: 39.1661 days
- Associations: month of Eyre, Fernia, Mark of Making
- Note: a forge-like moon favored by artificers and smiths; its light is described as pulsing like a bellows-fed fire.

### Dravago

- Title: The Herder's Moon
- Cycle: 44.1625 days
- Associations: month of Dravago, Risia, Mark of Handling
- Note: the largest moon by diameter, linked to farmers and herders and to a cold, steady, unchanging path.

### Nymm

- Title: The Crown
- Cycle: 49.7962 days
- Associations: month of Nymm, Daanvi, Mark of Hospitality
- Note: a golden, auspicious moon for celebration and feasting; described as uniquely sovereign among the twelve.

### Lharvion

- Title: The Eye
- Cycle: 56.1487 days
- Associations: month of Lharvion, Xoriat, Mark of Detection
- Note: the most unsettling moon, tied to seers and inquisitives and described as erratic, extreme, and difficult to understand.

### Barrakas

- Title: The Lantern
- Cycle: 63.3115 days
- Associations: month of Barrakas, Irian, Mark of Finding
- Note: a pale guiding light said to help seekers find what they pursue.

### Rhaan

- Title: The Book
- Cycle: 71.3881 days
- Associations: month of Rhaan, Thelanis, Mark of Scribing
- Note: a blue-white scholarly moon associated with writing, stories, and a patchwork surface like stitched pages.

### Sypheros

- Title: The Shadow
- Cycle: 80.495 days
- Associations: month of Sypheros, Mabar, Mark of Shadow
- Note: a dim, secretive moon linked to espionage, hidden knowledge, and encroaching ruin.

### Aryth

- Title: The Gateway
- Cycle: 90.7637 days
- Associations: month of Aryth, Dolurrh, Mark of Passage
- Note: a red-orange threshold moon associated with death, transition, and funeral observance.

### Vult

- Title: The Warding Moon
- Cycle: 102.3424 days
- Associations: month of Vult, Shavarath, Mark of Warding
- Note: the farthest and slowest moon, associated with defense, safety, and endurance; even its light can fail during Long Shadows.

## Dragonlance

Dragonlance uses three moons, with the system anchored by conjunction logic rather than independent per-moon anchors.

### Summary

| Moon | Title | Cycle (days) | Visibility | Association |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Solinari | The Silver Moon | 36 | Normal | Good magic, White Robes |
| Lunitari | The Red Moon | 28 | Normal | Neutral magic, Red Robes |
| Nuitari | The Black Moon | 8 | Hidden by default | Evil magic, Black Robes |

### Solinari

- Title: The Silver Moon
- Cycle: 36 days
- Visibility: normal
- Note: governs Good magic on Krynn and determines the power of White Robed wizards.

### Lunitari

- Title: The Red Moon
- Cycle: 28 days
- Visibility: normal
- Note: governs Neutral magic and matches the standard month length.

### Nuitari

- Title: The Black Moon
- Cycle: 8 days
- Visibility: hidden by default
- Note: governs Evil magic and is only visible to those aligned with darkness, especially Black Robed wizards.

## Exandria

Exandria uses a seeded moon model rather than fixed-cycle anchors.

### Summary

| Moon | Title | Base Cycle (days) | Cycle Mode | Visibility | Association |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Catha | The Guiding Light | 29 | `seeded_drift_uniform` | Normal | Sehanine, the Moonweaver |
| Ruidus | The Bloody Eye | 164 | `seeded_drift_triangular` | Visible window | Predathos-linked mystery |

### Catha

- Title: The Guiding Light
- Base cycle: 29 days
- Cycle formula: `29 + 1d11`
- Visibility: normal
- Note: Exandria's primary moon, associated with Sehanine; the implemented cycle drifts between 29 and 39 days.

### Ruidus

- Title: The Bloody Eye
- Base cycle: 164 days
- Cycle formula: `164 + 1d20 - 1d20`
- Visibility: visible window
- Note: appears full when visible, carries a strong ill omen, and is tied to the mystery of Predathos.
